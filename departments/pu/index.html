Courses

Arts

Arts is a stream which consists of interesting subjects such as Psychology, Sociology Education, Languages, Music, Economics, History, Geography, Political Science, etc. Arts courses in PUC is designed for students with strong linguistic, social, artistic, and interpersonal intelligence.

Courses offered
  • HEPS: History, Economics, Political Science and Sociology

Commerce

Commerce is a combination of Accounts, Business Studies with Mathematics/Statistics/ Economics/Computer Science stream. The course equips the candidates with in-depth knowledge about the theory and practical applications of the commerce concepts.

Courses offered
  • HEBA: History, Economics, Business Studies and Accountancy
  • CEBA: Computer Science, Economics, Business Studies and Accountancy

Science

Science is a combination of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics with Biology/Computer Science/ Electronics stream. The course equips the candidates with in-depth knowledge about the theory and practical applications of the science concepts.

Courses offered
  • PCMB: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology
  • PCMCs: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Computer Science
